# Hearth & Crumb

Hearth & Crumb is a warm, beginner-friendly sourdough starter tracker for home bakers who want to maintain feedings, ratios, and readiness in the browser.

## What it does

- Saves multiple sourdough starters and feeding entries with localStorage.
- Tracks starter, flour, and water amounts in grams.
- Calculates feeding ratios, hydration, and estimated peak timing.
- Helps users check readiness with rise percentage, bubbles, smell, and texture.
- Includes beginner-friendly sourdough recipes for when the starter is ready to bake.
- Gives friendly care guidance while leaving the final baking decision to the user.

https://your-github-username.github.io/hearth-and-crumb-app/

## How to use it

1. Add your sourdough starter and give it a name.
2. Log each feeding with gram amounts, room temperature, and notes.
3. Use the readiness check and baking prep tools before deciding whether to bake.

## Known limitations

- Data is saved only in the current browser, so it will not sync across devices.
- Clearing browser storage will remove saved starters, feedings, and readiness checks.
- Peak timing and readiness guidance are estimates; the baker should make the final decision.
- Recipes are starting suggestions and are not yet customizable inside the app.
